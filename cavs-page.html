Coming Home

Just recently, LeBron James made his decision to return to Cleveland after four years at Miami. This comes after a brutal loss to the San Antonio Spurs in the NBA Finals. The Spurs won dominantly four games to one. James seemed to be the best player on the Heat team, throughout the Finals. On 7/12/2014, LeBron sent a note to Sports Illustrated that he was leaving the Miami Heat, and joining the Cavaliers. Cleveland went crazy. It turned into the happiest place in the world. Hopefully, with the new coach, the talent we got in the offseason, and hard practice, the Cavs will go far this year.
